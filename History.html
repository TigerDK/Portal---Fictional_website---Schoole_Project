Aperture Science

Background Story (Spoiler-Free)

In a world driven by innovation, Aperture Science stands at the forefront of cutting-edge technology. Known for its groundbreaking research, the facility focuses on exploring the boundaries of science and human ingenuity. Deep within the Enrichment Center, a series of tests challenges the mind like never before. Armed with a revolutionary tool, test subjects embark on an extraordinary journey of discovery and problem-solving. Behind the scenes, however, lies a mystery waiting to be unraveled.

Timeline of the Story in Portal

1940s–1970s: The Birth of Aperture Science

Aperture Science begins as a small company focused on innovative products. Over time, it evolves into a cutting-edge research facility known for pushing the boundaries of science.

1970s–1980s: Ambition and Experimentation

The company shifts its focus to ambitious projects, including advanced portal technology. Their experiments promise to revolutionize the way people interact with space and technology.

1990s: A Turning Point

Aperture Science develops a state-of-the-art artificial intelligence to manage its facility. This marks a pivotal moment in the company's history, as the AI becomes integral to its operations.

The Enrichment Center:

The story takes place in the Aperture Science Enrichment Center, a vast, high-tech facility designed to test the limits of human ingenuity and problem-solving through a series of puzzles.

A New Test Subject:

You, the player, wake up in a mysterious testing facility. Guided by a calm and seemingly helpful voice, you are tasked with solving increasingly complex challenges using a revolutionary tool: the portal gun.

Mysteries and Challenges:

As you progress, you begin to notice that not everything is as it seems. Strange messages and hidden areas hint at a larger story behind the testing facility and its history.

A Journey of Discovery:

Your journey will take you beyond the test chambers and into the depths of the facility, revealing secrets and challenges that will test your wit, creativity, and resolve.

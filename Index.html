Welcome to Portal

Portal™ is a single-player game from Valve, set in the mysterious Aperture Science Laboratories. Praised as one of the most innovative games of its time, Portal offers players hours of unique and engaging gameplay.

About the Game

Portal is designed to change the way players think about and interact with their environment. Using the Aperture Science Handheld Portal Device, you’ll solve physics-based puzzles and challenges by opening portals to move objects and yourself through space. Similar to the groundbreaking mechanics introduced with Half-Life® 2's Gravity Gun, Portal redefines what’s possible in gaming.

You play as Chell, navigating a series of test chambers under the watchful eye of GLaDOS, Aperture Science’s darkly humorous AI. With every solved puzzle, you’ll uncover more about the secrets of Aperture Science and the eerie motives behind your tests.
